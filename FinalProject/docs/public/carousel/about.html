About the creators. It'll blow your mind.

While pondering ideas for the final project over freshly squeezed Grape Juice at the Data Viz Coctail Party, we decided to make a data viz using HALO 5 multiplayer stats. The viz explores correlations between players and teams as to why one team wins and the other loses. It all started with a sinking boat, barely keeping afloat. With their grades on the line, these two banned together so that this sinking boat would last till the end of the semester. And shockingly, we are still here.
